BEFORE YOU BEGIN

CODA helps you think. It does not replace your judgment.

When you dictate a clinical narrative describing a patient's death, CODA transcribes what you say and surfaces probabilistic causes of death in real time. All processing happens on your device.

The output is advisory. Final cause-of-death determination remains with you — and, where applicable, with post-mortem confirmation.

You can adjust preferences in Settings at any time.
